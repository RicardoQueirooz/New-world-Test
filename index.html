About the game:

New World is an MMO developed by Amazon Games Studio, and was released by Amazon on September 28th, 2021, for PC.

This wiki is a collaborative resource for the game and is maintained by the contributions of the fans. All editors are welcome.

Weapons

New world is a good game because it does not limit you to only one type of weapon, you can play with all the weapons you want, you can be a healer, tank or dps.

The only thing you need to do is to change your gear and attribute points, so that you can fill whatever role you wants.

Factions

In New World a faction can capture territories, and by capturing these territories you get lots of bonus, such as tax reduction, fast travel cost reduction and etc.

Expeditions

Expeditions are five-player instanced dungeons that will take players to the darkest corners of Aeternum.(Aeternum is the of the fictional region in the game)
